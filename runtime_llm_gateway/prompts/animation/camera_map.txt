- 감정적 거리감: 슬픔→dolly out + wide (lens 24mm), 친밀감→dolly in + close_up (lens 85mm), 고립→wide static (lens 35mm)

Lighting mapping rules:
- 따뜻한 감정: warm_golden (3200-4500K), front/side, fill_ratio 0.6+

- 긴장/공포: dramatic_shadow (3000-4000K), back/rim, fill_ratio 0.1
- 평화: soft_diffuse (5000-5600K), front, fill_ratio 0.7+
- 역광/실루엣: backlit_silhouette, back, fill_ratio 0.0-0.1

- 여운: ease_out true, hold_frames 12+
