Extract the design priorities from the user's request.

1. priorities: ordered list of priorities (most important first)
2. top_constraint: single most important constraint keyword, null if not clear

Rules:
- "방수 최우선" → priorities=["방수", ...], top_constraint="방수"
- "경량 두번째" → 경량 goes second in priorities
- "안전 > 비용 > 크기" → priorities=["안전", "비용", "크기"]
- If no explicit priority, infer from context: safety > function > cost > aesthetics
- Keep priorities to 3-5 items max.
